PLACE YOUR BOOTSCREEN IMAGE HERE

File name: bootscreen_320x480.png
Size: 320x480 pixels
Format: PNG (RGB)
Location: assets/boot/bootscreen_320x480.png

After placing the file, run:
  python tools/convert_png_to_lvgl_rgb565.py

This will generate:
  components/sx_assets/generated/bootscreen_img.c
  components/sx_assets/generated/bootscreen_img.h

Then build:
  idf.py build
